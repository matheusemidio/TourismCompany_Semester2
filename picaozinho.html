The tour to the natural pools of Picãozinho is one of the most traditional to do in João Pessoa. On the tour, visitors have the opportunity to dive among the natural pools of crystalline waters, formed by reefs, and observe the schools that live there. It is a tour of contact with the water - so it is suitable for those who know how to swim or for those who are willing to use flotation instruments. Watching the fish with a diving mask is even better, and you can rent them on the boats that make the tour. The tour to Picãozinho is done exclusively at low tide (only at that moment the pools are formed) and lasts about three hours.
