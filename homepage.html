João Pessoa is a Brazilian city, capital and main financial and economic center of the state of Paraíba. With a population, estimated in 2018, of 800 323 inhabitants, the capital of Paraíba is the eighth most populous city in the Northeast Region and the 23rd in Brazil, being, in its state, the most populous city. It is known as "Porta do Sol", due to the fact that Ponta do Seixas is located in the city, which is the easternmost point in the Americas, which makes the city known as the place "where the sun first rises on the American continent".
