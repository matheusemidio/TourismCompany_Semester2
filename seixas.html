Located just 500m from the coast of Praia do Seixas, close to the Easternmost Point of the Americas, the Seixas Pools are an immense bank of corals, covered by sea water, which are visible only when the tide is low, forming natural pools of warm, clear water that allows you to view colorful fish and other sea animals. An invitation to dive into a true ecological paradise, nicknamed the Brazilian Caribbean.
